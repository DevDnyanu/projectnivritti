PROJECT NIVRITTI

The word "Nivritti" is derived from Sanskrit and meaning "withdrawal". Project Nivritti is a youth-led initiative dedicated to breaking the cycle of substance abuse through awareness, empathy, and education.

Meet the Founder

The Project Nivritti was started by a 17-year-old who wished to address the issue of substance abuse amongst teenagers in India. The aim of this project is to reach out to schools and conduct drives for students aged 13 to 17 and educate them about the harms of abusing substances.
