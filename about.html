About the Project

Making a Real Impact

We strive to do better for the youth community by conducting Anti-Substance Abuse awareness sessions for students of Grades 9 to 12.

Our Story

Started in March 2025 by Aditya Gunjal - an alumnus of TribesForGood (an international school for social impact volunteers) and a qualified orator with an Associate's Diploma in Public Speaking from Trinity College London.

Having been trained to address sensitive issues with care and responsibility, I have previously conducted sessions at Euro School Wakad and with Kshamata Foundation, where it was well-received.

The session is designed to be informative, age-appropriate, and interactive, focusing on prevention, resilience, and informed decision-making.
